  1. Takeaway Service Hours: Monday to Sunday and public holidays from 2 pm to 10 pm
  2. Please place your order at least 2 hours before the meal pick-up time on the same day. Staff will confirm by phone on the day of meal pick-up. If you do not receive a phone confirmation, please call the branch to inquire. Once confirmed, you can pick up at the branch at the scheduled time.
